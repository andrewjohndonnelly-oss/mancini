Who We Are

The largest food broker in the Intermountain West.

Mancini Rodon Group was built on a simple idea: if you take care of people — the manufacturers you represent and the retailers you serve — success follows. That belief has guided us for over four decades.

We service over 500 grocery stores, 1,000 convenience stores, 50 mass merchandisers, and 100 drug stores — with offices in Salt Lake City, Boise, and Billings so we're always close to the market we know best.
