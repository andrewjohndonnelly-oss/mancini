Who We Are

The largest food broker in the Intermountain West.

Mancini Rodon Group was built on a simple idea: if you take care of people — the manufacturers you represent and the retailers you serve — success follows. That belief has guided us for over four decades.

We service over 500 grocery stores, 1,000 convenience stores, 50 mass merchandisers, and 100 drug stores — with offices in Salt Lake City, Boise, and Billings.

Why Mancini Rodon

Four decades of earned trust.

Family-owned, regionally rooted

We've operated in the Intermountain West for over 40 years. We know the retailers, the buyers, and the markets — not from a national playbook, but from decades of showing up.

Real relationships, real doors

Our retailer relationships are personal. When we call Associated Food Stores, WinCo, or Albertsons, they pick up — because we've been doing business together for years.

Proven track record

12+ Broker of the Year awards from manufacturers like Clif Bar, Ghirardelli, and Lindt. That recognition reflects a standard of execution our manufacturers count on.

National alliance, local advantage

As part of a national brokerage alliance, we bring national reach and resources while maintaining the local expertise and agility that regional markets demand.

Full-service execution

From first sales call to final shelf placement — we handle sales, marketing, merchandising, and consulting so you can focus on making great products.
